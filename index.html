About

MITeri is the Nepali student association at MIT. It was founded in 2014 to connect students at MIT interested in Nepal and Nepali culture. We have about 20 members today including undergraduates and graduates.

nepalS

Biweekly gathering of Nepalese students in Boston area.

Every two week or so, Nepalese students from MIT, Wellesley, Tufts, Harvard and other neighbouring university meet together to discuss recent and relevant topics relating to Nepal, Nepalese and the whole world. Our past topics have included history of Nepal, current political climate of Nepal etc. This is an informal gathering where we encourage sharing of opinions and knowledge about Nepal not limiting to socio-economic, or geopolitical discussions.
If you are interested, contact us through facebook, email or any of our members at any of the universities mentioned above. We will be more than happy to have you in our meetings and learn more from you.

  • Date: Biweekly,
  • Location: We try new location every time.
